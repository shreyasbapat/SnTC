About Us

SnTC, short for, Science and Technology Council is IIT Mandi's Technical Society. It is a body of students advised by faculty. SnTC constitutes of following clubs:

  1. Robotronics Club
  2. Space Technology and Astronomy Cell (STAC)
  3. Energy Club
  4. Programming Club
  5. Nirmaan Club
  6. Society for Collaborative Research and Innovation (SCRI)
  7. SAE Collegiate
  8. IEEE Chapter
  9. ACM Chapter

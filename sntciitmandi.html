About Us


  SnTC, short for, Science and Technology Council is IIT Mandi's Technical Society. It is a body of students advised by faculty. SnTC constitutes of following clubs:
  1) Robotronics Club
  2) Space Technology and Astronomy Cell (STAC)
  3) Energy Club
  4) Programming Club
  5) Nirmaan Club
  6) Society for Collaborative Research and Innovation (SCRI)
  7) SAE Collegiate
  8) IEEE Chapter
  9) ACM Chapter
                                                                   Each club is coordinated by two coordinators and comprises of students of similar interests.
                                                         Each club is advised by Faculty Advisor and Co-Advisor. All the clubs are open for all students of IIT Mandi.
                                                                                      However, a core team is formed for efficient running of club.
